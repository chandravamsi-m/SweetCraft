Frequently Asked

How far in advance should I order?

For custom wedding cakes, we recommend booking 3-6 months in advance. For smaller milestone celebration cakes, 1-2 weeks is typically sufficient.

Do you offer delivery & setup?

Yes, we provide climate-controlled delivery within a 50-mile radius. For multi-tiered wedding cakes, we perform professional on-site setup and staging at your venue.

Can I request vegan or gluten-free options?

Absolutely! We bake premium gluten-free and vegan alternative options for most of our standard flavor profiles using dedicated pans and organic ingredients.
